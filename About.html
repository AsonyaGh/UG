About EcoChat

EcoChat is a cutting-edge waste management solution designed to simplify how residential and commercial clients request services like waste bin delivery and collection. Through a chatbot interface, EcoChat offers an efficient way for users to engage with waste management services, learn about recycling, and support a sustainable environment.

Our mission is to bring convenience to waste management by connecting users with waste services in an easy, conversational way, all while helping to protect the environment.

We provide a variety of services including waste bin requests, complaint management, recycling tips, and waste management consultancy. Our goal is to ensure that waste management becomes a smooth and hassle-free experience for everyone.
